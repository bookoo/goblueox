May Giveaway! Win a $50 Gift Card on Blue Ox Heating & Air’s Facebook Page
May 7, 2015

Despite the fact that spring weather has been all over the place this year, we’re excited for the warmth we know is right around the corner. From walks around the lake the Great Minnesota Get-Together, there’s so much to do during summer months in the Twin Cities.

Throughout the month of May, we’ll be sharing some of our favorite things to do during summer in the Twin Cities. With each post, we’ll be asking you to share some of your favorite things to do! The best part? Respondents will have a shot at winning a $50 gift card!

Here’s how it works:

From Thursday, May 7 to Thursday, May 28, we’ll regularly post our favorite summer activities on our Facebook page. Here’s how to respond for a chance to win:

  • Like’ our Page
  • ‘Like’ the trivia question post
  • Comment on the trivia question post with your answer

We’ll give you 48 hours to respond. Then, we’ll select a random winner from the respondents! If you won, you’ll receive a private ‘Congratulations’ message from us and more details about the $50 gift card coming your way!

Here are some nitty-gritty fine-print specifics:

Limit one $50 gift card per participant for the duration of the contest. Gift cards will be awarded during the contest period and delivered to winners through means agreed upon by individual winners and Blue Ox Heating & Air. No purchase or consideration necessary. ‘Liking’ and/or answering (via “Comment”) is not necessary for contest entry. As an Alternate Method of Entry, participants may send a Facebook message to Blue Ox Heating & Air to enter for a chance to win a particular drawing in lieu of answering the associated question post on Facebook. Only one comment per respondent per post will be considered for entry. Anyone found to be using multiple accounts for contest entry will be ineligible. Limited to persons over the age of 18 who are legal residents of the State of Minnesota. Void where prohibited. Blue Ox Heating & Air reserves the right in its absolute discretion to disqualify any entrant if it has reasonable grounds to believe that an entrant has breached any of these rules or any applicable law. Gift card terms and conditions apply.
